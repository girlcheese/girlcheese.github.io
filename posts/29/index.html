Clerkenwell Burrito Diaries - Freebird Exmouth Market

Sep 30th, 2010 12:17 pm

I'd already eaten more than half way through when I realised I should take a photo. The Carnitas was back on and definitely up to standard; juicy, flavoursome and tender. Rice was lovely, and reminded me again why I love Freebird above all others. But unfortunately the beans were a little wet and the burrito was packed a little loosely. Perhaps due to the new staff member on frontline duties? Who knows.

Either way - service with a smile, quick without hurry, hot and tasty with lovely guacamole. 

Still the best option in EC1. In my humble and well satisfied opinion.
