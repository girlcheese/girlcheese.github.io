Festinho Get-in: Day One

Aug 23rd, 2010 9:09 pm

A stylish drive into the countryside and we arrived just in time to
get the tents up in daylight, and enjoy a lovely Gin & Tonic with the
sunset. What a lovely location. I haven't managed to help much today,
but will no doubt be packing the daylight hours tomorrow with plenty
of jobs. It looks like the lucky folk first on site have been busy
already though, the main stage is going in and the first stakes are
down in the party camping field. Many more jobs are underway but this
does feel like the calm before the storm!

I don't want to spoil any of the wonderful surprises the place will
bring when you arrive. You are coming right? It's beginning to be the
site it soon will become. Festinho a-go-go.
